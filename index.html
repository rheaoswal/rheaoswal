5th Harmony

Introduction

5th Harmony was an American girl group composed of 5 girls named Ally Brooke, Normani Kordei, Dinah Jane, Lauren Jauregui and Camila Cabello. They all met on the TV show "X Factor" where they signed a record deal with Simon Cowell. Since then, they have put out mutliple albums, gone on tour numerous times and created a fan base all over the world.

Reflection
Reflection was 5th Harmony's first album and included collaborations with big starts and producers including Tyga and Dr. Luke. It was pretty sucessful as it entered the Billboard 200 at #5 and even allowed them to go on tour with it.
Seven Twenty-Seven 7/27 is their second studio album and it features the American rappers Ty Dolla Sign, Fetty Wap and Missy Elliot. It debuted #4 on the US Billboard 200 and all their singles placed as well.
Fifth Harmony is their self-titled third studio album which included features by Gucci Mane and producers like Ammo and Srillex. It also charted in the top tens of many countries including Canada, New Zealand and the United Kingdom.
